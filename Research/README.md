# Research

> Research informs product, architecture, and governance decisions. It is never mixed into product documents — it lives here and is referenced from there.
> A research note is not a decision. When research leads to a decision, write a GDR, ADR, or PDR. Link back to the research that supported it.

---

## Folders

| Folder | Contents |
|---|---|
| `Competitors/` | Competitor analysis, market maps, positioning notes |
| `Technology/` | Technology evaluations — AI models, LLMs, frameworks, platforms |
| `Nutrition/` | Nutritional science, dietary guidelines, food safety standards |
| `Regulations/` | Privacy law (GDPR, CCPA), AI regulation, food/health regulations by jurisdiction |
| `Ideas/` | Founder notes, early-stage hypotheses, unstructured thinking |

---

## How to Use This Folder

- **Add notes freely.** Research doesn't need to be polished. It needs to be findable.
- **Link from decisions.** When a GDR, ADR, or PDR is informed by research here, add a `research:` field to the frontmatter pointing to the relevant file.
- **Mark status.** A note can be `active` (ongoing), `concluded` (led to a decision), or `archived` (superseded or no longer relevant).
- **Never copy content into product documents.** Reference it. The research file is the source of truth for its findings.
